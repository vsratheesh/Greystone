Investment Banking

“Thinking about sale or purchase of a stock or bond?"

Investment banking at Graystone assists in large, complicated financial transactions to entrepreneurs, corporations and investors. It includes financial advice to structure a deal, issuing security as a means to raise money and documentation.

Our expert team enables you to navigate with the large enterprises, advising on how to best structure the deals. The organisation has successfully advised on transactions worth more than INR 2, 500 Cr.

Private Equity Advisory

Graystone holds leadership in the PE market sector since its origin. We have profound knowledge in advising promoters and companies at both fund raising and portfolio analysis exercises.

Wherein we apply an enterprise approach for lending practices in risk management for private equity in accordance with the ever changing business environment.

Our strength lies in our relationship with Private equity funds based on a model for performance improvement by implementing effective approaches.

M & A Advisory

Interested in expansion through acquisition?

Acquiring a healthy company with a good culture shoots your market share, revenues and profits. It helps identify synergies in the market and integrating them in your company so as to expedite growth.

M & A advisory at Graystone help corporate looking for guidance in their business expansion. Our team helps clients in evaluating the commercial benefits and risks of a transaction, business valuation advice, optimal structuring of the acquisition and working with legal advisor.

Equity Capital Market

Equity capital market is the market lying between financial institution and the information provided by companies regarding their current financial situations and estimates of future performance.

Within the company’s investment banking division, Equity capital market is one of the most sturdy and powerful business domain. Here at Graystone, we have created a very dominant ECM practice.

The ECM team at Graystone helps our clients execute their primary market transactions by providing them with innovative structure, syndication, marketing and distribution.

Our strength lies in our deep industry insight and experience with leading business houses, corporate, banks and public sector undertakings.
