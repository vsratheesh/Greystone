Overview

Graystone Group has a strong presence across the globe and is one amongst the leading financial consulting firms and Non- Banking Financial Corporations (NBFC) attracting substantial investors, providing focused and transparent solutions.


The group was founded in June 2016 by Mr. Sayed Adnan and Mr. Hesham Mohamed Ali Janahi with the vision to create a platform for customers that enables them to find the right product fit in the market. Graystone equips its customers with easy access to information on all loan related products available in the market so as to secure the best deal both in terms of interest rates and loan value.

  • Credit Business - Wholesale Credit comprising of Structured Collateralized Credit to Corporate
  • Real Estate finance
  • Distressed Assets Credit
  • Retail Credit - Loan against property, LAS, SME and Agri Finance, and Rural Finance
  • Non-Credit Business - Capital Markets, Wealth Management, Asset Management and Agri Services.

Mission

Our mission is to pioneer access to capital by providing the best solutions in the finance industry through long term stakeholder value creation based on Leadership with Trust, Discretion and Excellence.

Vision

To become a global leader and strive for excellence in every business domain by creating an impact and improving the quality of life of the communities we serve keeping our core values intact
